Work

Postmates - Exploratory Product Analysis

At Postmates, I was responsible for conducting exploratory analysis to help determine business levers Growth, Marketing, Product, and Engineering could leverage to facilitate growth and increase market share. One of the main projects I worked on was utilizing the Yelp API in order to analysis inventory gaps for restaurant onboarding.

Data Driven Detroit - Redesign of Training Module

With infoLab at the University of Michigan's School of Information, I collaborated with a team of Data Science Consultants, User Experience Consultants, and User Researchers to redesign and create a user-friendly training module for an online data analysis tool, State of the Detroit Child. One of our goals was to take the original training module which was a single scroll page and make it more dynamic and interactive with the users.

University of Michigan - Creating a Hollistic College Application

Working with the University of Michigan's Office of Enrollment Management as a client, my team and I created a supplemental database that provided valuable information on school districts and counties. The goal was to help Michigan move towards a more hollistic recruiting approach and understand the whole makeup of potential students. As of right now, we are currently developing the databases.

University of Michigan - Informational Graphic Design

During my time at the School of Information, I was able to take a graphic design course that focused on conveying information in impactful and meaningful ways. We were able to utilize Adobe Creative Cloud Applications (e.g. Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.) to create user personas, high fidelity wireframes, infographics, and logos.
